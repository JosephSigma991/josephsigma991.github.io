Featured Project
IntelligenceOps

Problem: Ops teams cannot quickly identify which stations, delay categories, and codes drive departure delays. Separating what's controllable from what's inherited takes too long. Accountability stays vague.

Solution: A two-layer system. SQL Server ETL pipeline publishing validated CSV artifacts, consumed by an 8-page Streamlit dashboard. IATA-aligned KPIs, Pareto analysis, what-if scenario simulation with regression-backed OTP estimates, and automated PDF executive reports. A CI pipeline validates a 13-file data contract across 3 time grains on every commit.

Result: Top delay drivers surface by station in seconds. Controllable and inherited delays separate at code level. The 2-3 categories that own 70%+ of total minutes are visible before the morning meeting starts. Scenario simulation gives management data-backed estimates instead of guesses.
